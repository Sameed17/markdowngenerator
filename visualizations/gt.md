## III Effects of next-nearest-neighbor interactions on entanglement

We have studied the effects of finite temperature and magnetic fields on entanglement, and now consider the model containing two kinds of spins, spin \(\frac{1}{2}\) and \(1\), alternating on a ring with antiferromagnetic exchange coupling between both the NN spins and the NNN spins.
